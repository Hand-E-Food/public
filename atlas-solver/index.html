Instructions

  • Left click a track to place or remove a track.
  • Right click a track to place or remove a train station.
  • The Build tracks shows surplus tracks: tracks that are connecting cities that are already connected by a shorter route.
  • The Longest track scores after you place at least one track.
  • Tickets turn green when complted.
  • Long tickets turn red if another long ticket is completed. Red tickets do not add to the total score.
  • The numbers on each track are the number of pieces required to build that track.
  • Tracks showing x+y require x engines and y other cards to build.
  • Floating the mouse over a ticket highlights the cities it uses.
  • Floating the mouse over a city highlights all tickets that use that city.
  • The number on each city indicates the total points from completed tickets that use this city, so you can see that city's value. Long tickets are not included in this total.
